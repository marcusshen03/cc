Event Details

This year, Cub Council is excited to be hosting a Christmas Card Making Event. We've teamed up with 360Kids to provide you with the opportunity to make Christmas cards for at-risk children and youth.

Here's how to participate:
1. Make your cards at home. Please note that there is n o limit on the number of cards that you can make, but you will receive a maximum of 5 volunteer hours . Additionally, cards made with little to no effort (i.e., printing cards from online) will not be accepted for volunteer hours.
2. Attach a sticky note with your full name, grade, and homeroom teacher to the front of each card.
3. Drop your cards in the bin in the main hall across from the main office on any day from Friday, December 10th, 2021 to Wednesday, December 15th, 2021 .
4. Optional: Although we will carefully go through each card, if you would like to ensure your volunteer hours are recorded appropriately, please fill out the following Google Form ( https://forms.gle/eFZrJfoxwRJXLHLp6 ).
5. Have fun!
​
​​Spread love, joy, and hope this holiday season by making Christmas cards for youth across Ontario!​
